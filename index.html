About VJTI

Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI) is an engineering college located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India, and one of the oldest engineering colleges in Asia. Founded in 1887 and formerly known as the Victoria Jubilee Technical Institute, it adopted its present name in 1998.[1] VJTI is an academically and administratively autonomous institute, however it is a part of the University of Mumbai and its degrees and diplomas are issued by the university.
